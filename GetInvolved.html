Get Involved

What can YOU do to help prevent climate change ?

  • Reduce the use of plastic bags and start recycling and reusing.
  • Start to use eco-friendly products instead of plastic bags.
  • Stop the chopping of trees and start protecting them.
  • Reduce the use of vehicles and walk or cycle which would allow you to stay healthy physically as well as, mentally.
  • Cut down energy usage.
  • Use energy sensibly.
  • Project your voice to others so they also contribute in stopping climate change.
  • Reuse energy by installing solar panels.
  • Plant more trees.
 

What organizations are helping to stop climate change ?

There are many organizations that are helping to stop climate change. All those organizations have a mission that is to solve the problems caused by climate change. Some organizations even collaborate with one another and with the people worldwide and share what they are doing through social media platforms to make more people contibute and make the world a safe and healthy place for the future. The names of some organizations are:

  1. Union of Concerned Scientists
  2. Center of Climate and Energy Solutions
  3. Citizens Climate Lobby
  4. Climate Action Network
  5. Green Climate Fund
  6. World Meteorological Organization
